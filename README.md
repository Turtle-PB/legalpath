## 🎯 Our Mission

> **LegalPath exists to democratize legal access for everyone — regardless of income, background, or circumstance.**
>
> We believe that justice should not be reserved for those who can afford it. Every person deserves to understand their rights, navigate the legal system with confidence, and advocate for themselves when the system feels overwhelming.
>
> LegalPath provides a free, private, self-contained legal toolkit that puts the power of legal knowledge directly into the hands of the people who need it most — workers facing unfair treatment, immigrants seeking citizenship, consumers fighting predatory businesses, individuals with disabilities navigating benefits systems, and survivors rebuilding their lives.
>
> **No paywalls. No data harvesting. No subscriptions. No server.**
>
> Everything runs on your device. Your data stays yours. Your story stays private.
>
> We built this because the legal system is designed to be intimidating — and intimidation is the enemy of justice. LegalPath breaks down that barrier by translating complex legal concepts into plain English, generating professional documents in minutes, and providing step-by-step guidance through every legal process from EEOC charges to bankruptcy filings.
>
> **Knowledge is power. LegalPath gives that power back to the people.**

## 🔬 Document Analysis Engine

Upload any legal document (NDA, release, severance agreement, medical record) and LegalPath's 7-layer analysis engine scans for:

1. **Clause Detection** — 38 legal clause types with 400+ keyword patterns
2. **Gap Analysis** — 10 checks for missing provisions
3. **Loophole Detector** — 8 exploitable weaknesses
4. **ADA Violation Detector** — 6 federal disability law violations
5. **Medical Misconduct Detector** — 4 malpractice patterns
6. **Statute of Limitations** — 5 deadline alerts
7. **Health Score** — 0-100 position assessment

## 🛡️ Privacy & Security

- **100% Local** — No server, no cloud, no tracking
- **IndexedDB** — Documents stored in browser's local database
- **localStorage** — Settings and notes saved locally
- **Password Gate** — App protected with configurable password
- **No Accounts** — No sign-up, no login, no data collection
- **PWA** — Installable on Android, iPhone, iPad, Windows
- **Offline** — Service worker caches everything for offline use
